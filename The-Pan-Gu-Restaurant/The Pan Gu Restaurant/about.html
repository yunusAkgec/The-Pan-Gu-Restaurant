Who We Are ?

As Leyong Family, we have served far eatern dishes since 1964. Our founder is Kim Keu Leyong who is started the Pan Gu Restaurant as mobile local food shop in Chinatown, Manhattan. Kim Keu, prepared his dishes with fresh meat and vegetables. His mobile vehicle was so clean and elegant. People often said to Kim, "Your bussiness is going very well, you are so cook delicious meals. Why do not start your own restaurant?" However, Kim Keu has already three children and he was considering the restaurant bussiness too for pretend to be. Hereby, first restaurant opened in near East Cost Harbor, Manhattan. Restaurant's name "Pan Gu" is referred to Antique Chinese creator god. After eight successful bussiness year Leyong family moved the restaruant to it's current location: Chinatown. Since 1972, "The Pan Gu Restaurant" cook dishes gentle, clean and super-delicious beacuse of their respect to humanity, eastern culture and cuisine. Last decade, Leyong family wants to start a eastern cuisine academy by name "Pan Gu Cooking Academy". That way, family teach the people about eastern culture, train for cooking techniques and methods. If you wondering the academy, please mail us. Our contact information are below.
